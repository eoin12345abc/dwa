The blocks on this page are asynchronous, based upon the aperiodic property of prime numbers.

Given a row number N, the time it takes the block to move across it is P(N) seconds, where P(N) is the Nth prime number. (I let P(1)=1.)

Furthermore, the time it takes the colors to change for row N is P(N+2)/2 seconds.

Therefore we will get asynchronous color change and block alignment, since no two prime numbers are divisible by each other.
